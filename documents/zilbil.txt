                                          Zilbil Clothing Store Policies

1. Refund Policy

1.1 Eligibility for Refunds

Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase.
Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached.
Proof of purchase is required for all returns.
1.2 Non-Refundable Items

Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.
Gift cards are non-refundable.
Items that have been damaged cannot be returned unless until they recieved it damaged from the box when they opened
Custom or personalized clothing items are non-refundable.
1.3 Process for Returns

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at [Customer Service Email] with your order number and reason for return.
Once your return is approved, you will receive a return shipping label.
Please pack the items securely and include the original packing slip.
1.4 Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive your return.
Refunds will be issued to the original method of payment.
Shipping costs are non-refundable.
2. Sale Policies

2.1 Discounts and Promotions

Discounts and promotional offers cannot be combined with other discounts unless stated otherwise.
Promotional codes must be entered at the time of purchase to receive the discount.
2.2 Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns or exchanges.
Please review your order carefully before purchasing final sale items.
3. Shipping Policy

3.1 Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.
3.2 Shipping Methods

We offer standard, expedited, and overnight shipping options.
Shipping times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected.
3.3 International Shipping

We ship to select international destinations.
International customers are responsible for customs fees and import duties.

4.1 Information Collection

We collect information you provide when you create an account, place an order, or contact us.
4.2 Use of Information

We use your information to process orders, improve our services, and communicate with you.
4.3 Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information.
5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

5.1 How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your order on our website under "Order Tracking."
5.2 Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or canceled within 24 hours of purchase. Please contact customer service as soon as possible.
5.3 What do I do if I receive a defective item?

If you receive a defective item, please contact customer service with your order number and a photo of the defect. We will provide a replacement or refund.
5.4 Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional fee. You can select this option at checkout.
